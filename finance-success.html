Banking & Finance - Infrastructure Modernization

One of the leading private banks in the Middle East was struggling with outdated legacy systems that couldn't scale, resulting in delayed transaction settlements and inefficient CI/CD practices.

CloudNexis migrated the entire infrastructure to Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS), set up a GitHub Actions CI/CD pipeline, and integrated Azure Key Vault for secure secrets management.

The transformation reduced deployment time by 85%, brought agility to development teams, and cut down yearly infrastructure costs by 40%. Customers noticed faster services, and internal audits praised the compliance-friendly design.
