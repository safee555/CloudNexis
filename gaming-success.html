Gaming Industry - Success Story

A leading mobile gaming startup was facing severe server lag and resource throttling during peak gameplay hours. Users were experiencing disconnections, high latency, and poor reviews started affecting app store rankings.

The company’s AWS-based architecture was not optimized. Costs were rising, yet performance remained poor due to improper autoscaling, lack of CDN, and missing load balancers.

CloudNexis stepped in to redesign the infrastructure:

  • Configured EC2 with proper autoscaling groups
  • Deployed AWS CloudFront for global content delivery
  • Implemented a hybrid Redis + Aurora DB layer to reduce read latency

Within a week, the server uptime improved from 89% to 99.99% and app ratings jumped. The startup was able to retain its customer base and launch its next multiplayer feature confidently.
