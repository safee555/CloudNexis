Healthcare Sector - EMR System Modernization

A hospital chain with over 30 branches was using outdated EMR systems with security vulnerabilities. Daily outages resulted in critical patient delays and HIPAA compliance violations, risking fines and trust.

CloudNexis implemented AWS HealthLake and Amazon Aurora with encrypted backups, and connected real-time monitoring via CloudWatch and GuardDuty. Data was anonymized and made available securely for authorized teams.

System uptime rose from 89% to 99.99%, compliance audits were passed without findings, and patient satisfaction increased as EMR performance improved drastically.
