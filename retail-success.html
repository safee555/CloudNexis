Retail Industry - AI Based Inventory Optimization

A growing retail chain was facing unpredictable stock levels and excess warehouse costs due to manual forecasting. Their regional stores were often out of fast-moving items.

CloudNexis deployed AWS Lambda and Amazon Forecast for predictive analytics. Data from POS systems was synced to Amazon S3 for training, and dashboards were created in QuickSight for managers.

The result was a 35% reduction in overstock, a 25% improvement in item availability, and millions saved in operational logistics. Customer experience in stores dramatically improved.
